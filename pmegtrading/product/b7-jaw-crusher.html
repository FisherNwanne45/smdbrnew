Product Information

Specifications

  Transport dimensions Working dimensions
Weight 69.1 t (76 sT)  
Length 15.293 mm (50’2”) 16.840 mm (55’3”)
Height 3.545 mm (11’8″) 4.325 mm (14’2”)
Width 3.130 mm (10’3”) 5.800 mm (19′)

Performance Highlights

  • Feed size up to 700 mm (28”)
  • Vibrating feeder with 2step grizzly 2.200 mm x 1.080 mm (7’3″ x 3’7″)
  • Feed opening 1.200 x 830 mm (47” x 32,7”)
  • C.S.S. min. – max. 75 – 250 mm (3″ – 10”)
  • Capacity up to 600 tonnes / hour
  • Full hybrid electric driven, energy saving version available
  • Removable feed hopper and feeder with scalper for transport
  • Intake hopper suitable for wheel loader and excavator, 6 m³ (7,8 yard³)
  • Smart sequential auto start/stop from remote
  • Ability to track while producing
  • 2 speed track drive
  • Extensive option list to configure to your application
  • Removable engine /generator compartment

Applications

  • Quarrying and mining
  • Granite, Basalt and medium and hard stone
  • Primary crushing
  • Concrete and landfill recycling
  • Construction waste
  • Demolition
  • Gravel
  • Coal, slag and others
